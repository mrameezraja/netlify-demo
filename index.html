FAQs

  • What is a scheduled webhook?

    If a webhook is scheduled for later date or it is recurring and not triggered yet then it is considered as scheduled webhook.

  • Does Cronhooks have a free plan?

    Yes, we do. With the free plan, you can schedule a single webhook. You can create a new one when older schedule is completed.

  • How do I upgrade to a paid plan?

    Go to "Plans & Billing" page and click on "Upgrade" button, then proceed accordingly.

  • How can I cancel my subscription?

    If you want to cancel your paid plan you can go to "Plans & Billing" page and click on "Cancel Subscription" button. Your plan will be canceled.

  • What if I need more schedules?

    No problem. Just send us an email and let us know what you need, then we will sort something out.
